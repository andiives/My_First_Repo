On Queue

When tasked with the intructions to create an app that solves a problem, the options are endless. According to thefactsite.com the average human spends about 5 years of their life waiting in line. We had the idea to slim down this statistic by tackling the time spent waiting in line at events, bars, clubs, and festivals.

Queue On Queue

After extensive research regarding general feelings about standing in line, the results were far from shocking.... Surprise! Nobody likes waiting. We found that in some cases people will even opt out of going to certain places alltogether if they see that there is a long line. On Queue is designed to relieve the stress and hassle of the wait and to save users from the FOMO attached to it.

Group Members: Andi Ives, Haley Knapp, Kaylin Price

Clickable Prototype

The On Queue logo is meant to portray the look of two people standing in line which also spells out "On Q". It follows the app's branding of bold black, contrasting with lime green elements.
